You were the Chosen One Acquire this title

She has been chosen as a child, preparing for the moment when she will have to fulfill her destiny. However, the arrival of a new priest at the temple changes his life forever. It is soon discovered that the prophecy was misinterpreted from the beginning and that the girl whom they all revered as savior is nothing but a fraud.

Stripped of her status and convinced that everything is part of a plan to end her, Arlie will decide to sneak up on the expedition that is searching for the new chosen one.

With the only company of her three-legged cat, a mysterious bard and the most powerful necklace in the world, the young woman will undertake an adventure that will take her to the most remote places and will teach her that destiny always has the capacity to surprise.

Forgive the Queen Acquire this title

In a society ruled by the most powerful, the only thing that maintains peace between classes are fear and the New Order, the system that was forged after the Great War and that is still in force in the Known World.

Unaware of the political clashes that threaten the balance within the imposing city-states, and too small to understand what is happening, Vayra horrified witness the kidnapping of her older sister. Without knowing it, she witnesses an instant that will change her life and the evolution of history forever.

Obsessed by Allera's disappearance, and unable to banish the guilt that has haunted her since that day, Vayra sets out to search for the impossible, a clue that tells her what might have happened to her. Hopeful, she travels the most hidden places in the world, not knowing that her path will lead her to the heart of an incipient revolt, and will lead her directly to face the worst of her nightmares, the truth.
